Rules

  • All games will be played on the website chess.com and players are expected to make an account on chess.com beforehand.
  • One can use their own PC, Laptop, or smartphone. Smartphone users can use the chess.com official app, and they can download it for free from the play store.
  • All the matches will be played in blitz mode and will be a knockout
  • All the matches before top 16 will be of 3|2 (3 min game with 2-sec increment after each move)
  • All the matches before top 4 will be of 5|5 (5 min game with 5-sec increment after each move)
  • Semi-Final and Final will be of 10 mins with no increment in time
  • There is no team. Only solo participation is available


Eligiblity Criteria

  1. Participant must be 18+.
  2. He/She should bring 2 passport size photos and an official identity proof.
  3. There is no refunding policy.
